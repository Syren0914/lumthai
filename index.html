Pad Thai

Pad Thai is a popular Thai stir-fried noodle dish. It typically includes flat rice noodles, protein (tofu, shrimp, or chicken), scrambled eggs, bean sprouts, peanuts, and lime. The flavors are a balance of sweet, salty, sour, and spicy, thanks to a sauce made from tamarind paste, fish sauce, and chili. Garnished with cilantro and lime, it's a versatile and flavorful dish.
