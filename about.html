Our Story

Thai Flavors in Virginia

Step into our inviting space adorned with traditional Thai decor, creating an atmosphere inspired by the bustling markets of Bangkok and the serene landscapes of Chiang Mai. Our chefs meticulously craft a menu that captures the essence of Thailand, featuring bold spices, aromatic herbs, and the perfect balance of sweet, sour, salty, and spicy.

Beyond the kitchen, Lum Thai is a community hub in Virginia, welcoming everyone to share in the joy of exceptional flavors. Whether you're a Thai cuisine enthusiast or a newcomer, our warm hospitality invites you to savor the taste of Thailand in the heart of Virginia.

Our Goals

At Lum Thai in Virginia, our goal is to create unforgettable culinary moments. We strive for excellence in every dish, presenting the true taste of Thailand in this vibrant state. Our warm hospitality aims to make every guest feel like a cherished member of our family. Lum Thai is not just a restaurant; it's a community hub where connections are made, stories are shared, and celebrations come to life. Mindful of our impact, we embrace sustainability, contributing positively to the community and the environment in Virginia. Join us on a journey of culinary discovery in Virginia. Lum Thai is about creating memories, exceeding your expectations, and inviting you to be a part of our story in the dynamic tapestry of Virginia's culinary scene.

Fresh and delicious traditional Thai food to delight the whole family.
